My Letter to Toffee


To my dear dog, Toffee:


I'm sorry I haven't come home in a while, I'm sure you must be worried about where I am and what I'm doing.

I assure you I will come home as soon as possible; when I can give you my full divided love and attention.

I miss you everyday, my sweet little munchkin and I hope you feel the same as well.

I hope Mom is taking care of you well while I'm not around; you better protect her as well.

Just know that I love you always, you and Mom, continue to stay safe.


- Bonnie
